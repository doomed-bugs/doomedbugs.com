Ortho Dial 'N Spray

  • Manufacturer: Ortho
  • Warrantary: 10 Years
Ortho Dial 'N Spray links:

    Main    

Ortho's Dial 'N Spray is the best solution for your concentrate dilution needs. A quick three-step process is all that is needed to start helping your plants and protecting them from pests. Dial 'N Spray is ideally used with any type of concentrate, whether it is a fertilizer like Miracle Grow, a pest control product like Ortho Bug-B-Gon MAX, a weed killer, or a fungicide. You don't need to worry about wasting any concentrate either.

How easy is it to use?

  1. Pour your concentrate product into the provided fill jar. You don't even need to measure out the concentrate! Just pour the concentrate into the jar until the jar is one-quarter to one-half full.
  2. Set dial the rate per gallon specified on the concentrate's label
  3. Spray the solution by adjusting the nozzle tip, turning on water, and squeezing the trigger to start the spraying
  4. After you are finished spraying, you may pour the unused concentrate back into its original container.

One of the best benefits of using the Dial 'N Spray is its economical design. You are able to reuse whatever concentrate that you don't initially use, and pour it back in the concentrate container. You can also easily save your last-used settings so you don't need to bother with them every time. The Dial 'N Spray saves you money and time!
